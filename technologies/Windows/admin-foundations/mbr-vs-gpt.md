# MBR vs GPT
MBR, Master Boot Record, is an older disk-type named after the boot sector located at the very beginning of a drive (the first sector) called MBR.

GPT, GUID Partition Table, is the newer standard than the MBR and was introduced as part of the UEFI initiative.
