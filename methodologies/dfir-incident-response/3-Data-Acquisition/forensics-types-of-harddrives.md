# forensics-types-of-harddrives
Here are four main types of hard drives and their characteristics mentioned: ("Types of hard drives 2024 | Which one to choose," 2024)

**1. Parallel ATA (PATA)**
Pros: Allows two devices to attach to the cable simultaneously
Cons: Large and difficult to manage
Use case: Outdated technology, not commonly used today

**2. Serial ATA (SATA)**
Pros: High capacity, low price
Cons: Limited data transfer speeds, mechanical parts prone to wear and tear
Use case: Common in desktop and laptop computers, suitable for general storage needs

**3. Small Computer System Interface (SCSI)**
Pros: Independent controller, widely applied, highly accessible
Cons: Costly, difficult to configure, performance degrades over time
Use case: Used in servers and high-performance computing environments

**4. Solid State Drive (SSD)**
Pros: Fast, safe, and durable with no moving parts
Cons: Costly, lower capacity compared to HDDs
Use case: Ideal for operating systems, gaming, and frequently accessed files

**5. NVMe, a newer version of SSD:**
Pros: Fastest reading and writing speed
Cons: Very expensive
Use case: Popular in gaming, video editing, and other intensive applications

When choosing a hard drive, the article recommends considering factors such as interface, capacity, price, power consumption, noise level, and life expectancy ("Types of hard drives 2024 | Which one to choose," 2024).
