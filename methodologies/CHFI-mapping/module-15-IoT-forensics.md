## Module 15: IoT Forensics

### Understand IoT Concepts
Internet of Things (IoT) devices span a diverse range of proprietary hardware platforms and architectures. Data is predominantly stored in internal **embedded flash memory**, creating extraction challenges due to a lack of standardization.

### Perform Forensics on IoT Devices
Investigations are heavily challenged by the sheer diversity of hardware, encrypted firmware images, and a lack of standardized forensic tools. Firmware images are frequently analyzed to locate hardcoded credentials or backdoors.

**Tools Used in Module 15:** Binwalk (firmware image extraction and filesystem analysis), Wireshark (network communications).
